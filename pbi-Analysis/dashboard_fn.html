Key Insights

  • 36% of all complaints are about Mortgage products — the single largest category by far.
  • Wells Fargo and JPMorgan Chase lead company-level volume, with Bank of America close behind at 1,162 cases.
  • California is the highest-volume state at 1,658 complaints, ahead of Texas and Florida.
  • 98.6% of complaints across all companies received a timely response — a strong industry compliance signal.
  • 2013 saw a sharp peak of 6,045 complaints, more than double every subsequent year combined average.
  • Most complaints close "with explanation" (59.6%) rather than with monetary or non-monetary relief.
