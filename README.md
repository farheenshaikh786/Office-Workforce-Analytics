# FinSight — Consumer Complaints Intelligence Dashboard

## 🎯 Project Overview
This project was designed and developed to analyze consumer complaint trends, demonstrating my capability to transform raw, complex data into actionable business intelligence. Leveraging my background in data operations and management, I managed the entire data workflow—from sourcing and structure verification to analytical visualization.

The primary goal of this analysis is to identify key problem areas, track regional complaint distributions, and understand company response times to improve overall customer satisfaction and operational efficiency.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Skills Demonstrated
*   **Data Visualization**: Microsoft Power BI
*   **Data Sourcing & Cleaning**: Excel / Power Query
*   **Core Skills**: Data Operations, Management, Business Intelligence, Data Analysis

---

## 📊 Dataset Profile
*   **Source**: Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB) — Publicly Available Records
*   **Volume**: 11,110 Complaints
*   **Time Period**: 2013 – 2017
*   **Key Attributes Managed**: Product, Sub-product, Company, State, Response Type, Timely Response, Submitted via

---

## 🔍 Key Insights & Business Analytics
*(Tip: Aapne dashboard mein jo main cheezein notice ki hain, unhe yahan 3-4 lines mein likh sakti hain. Ek generic example niche diya hai:)*

*   **Top Complaint Categories**: Identified specific products generating the highest volume of consumer disputes, allowing management to prioritize resource allocation.
*   **Operational Response**: Analyzed the percentage of complaints closed with a timely response to measure organizational efficiency.
*   **Geographical Trends**: Mapped complaint volumes across different states to pinpoint regional operational bottlenecks.
